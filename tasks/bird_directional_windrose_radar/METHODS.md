# Methods | 方法说明

## Objective | 目标
Quantify the directional pattern of bird new-distribution records relative to their previously known ranges, and visualize both the overall multi-order pattern and the order-specific directional signatures.
量化鸟类新纪录相对于既有分布范围的方向格局，并展示总体多目叠加模式及各目的方向特征。

## Workflow | 分析流程
1. Standardize province and species metadata, then derive directional categories using the cleaned bird record table.
2. Summarize counts across eight directions: North, Northeast, East, Southeast, South, Southwest, West, and Northwest.
3. Select major orders for the overlay figure and the 4x4 faceted panels.
4. Build overlay windrose and radar plots with order-specific colors and semi-transparent polygon fills.
5. Export plot-ready tables, figures, and a short result summary.

1. 标准化省份和物种元数据，并基于清洗后的鸟类新纪录表推导方向类别。
2. 按八个方向汇总记录数：North、Northeast、East、Southeast、South、Southwest、West、Northwest。
3. 选择主要目用于总体叠加图和 4x4 分面图。
4. 使用分目配色和半透明填充绘制叠加风玫瑰图与雷达图。
5. 导出作图数据、图件和结果摘要。

## Style Notes | 图形风格说明
- North is placed at the top and South at the bottom.
- Overlay figures follow the manuscript-style reference using colored outlines plus transparent polygon fills.
- Combined panels retain a compact 4x4 layout for visual balance.

- 北向置顶，南向置底。
- 总体叠加图参考论文样式，采用彩色边线与同色透明填充。
- 分面图保留紧凑的 4x4 布局，提升版式美观性。
