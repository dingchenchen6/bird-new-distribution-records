# Interpretation / 结果解释

## English

### Figure 3

The species-level analyses indicate that bird new records in China are strongly structured by species geography and mobility rather than by body size or broad conservation-risk grouping. In both the occurrence model and the negative-binomial frequency model, range size showed a clear positive effect. This suggests that species already occupying broader parts of China are more likely to generate additional provincial records, consistent with a combination of edge expansion, intermittent occupancy, and improved detection near previously under-documented range margins. Migration also emerged as an important positive factor: both partial migrants and full migrants had higher probabilities of generating new provincial records than resident or low-mobility species. By contrast, body mass and the coarse risk-group variable were not supported as strong predictors in the present bird dataset.

Taken together, Figure 3 supports the interpretation that for birds, new provincial records are especially associated with taxa that are mobile and already broadly distributed. In other words, the current bird new-record process appears to reflect not only hidden rarity being uncovered, but also active redistribution and detectability advantages among widespread migratory taxa.

### Figure 4

The province-level analysis shows that current survey effort is a key positive driver of discovery intensity once new-record counts are standardized by province area. Provinces with stronger current observation/reporting intensity have markedly higher log-transformed new-record density. Province area shows a strong negative effect in the same model, indicating that large provinces tend to have lower discovery intensity per unit area even when their absolute record totals may be high. Historical survey effort, per capita GDP, and habitat heterogeneity did not show strong independent effects in the final primary model, although GDP retained a moderate share of explained variance in the hierarchical partitioning analysis.

Relative-importance analysis further clarifies the structure of the province-level signal. Area contributes the largest share of explained variance, followed by current survey effort, whereas historical effort and habitat heterogeneity contribute little independent explanatory power. The sensitivity model using a count response with an area offset yields qualitatively similar conclusions in that effort- and structure-related terms remain the dominant explanatory axes. Overall, the province-level results reinforce the view that bird new-record accumulation is jointly shaped by observation-process intensity and spatial scale constraints.

## 中文

### Figure 3

物种层分析表明，中国鸟类新纪录主要受物种地理属性和迁移能力驱动，而不是主要受体型或粗尺度保护风险组驱动。在出现模型和负二项频次模型中，分布范围均表现出稳定的正效应。这说明，已经在中国更广泛分布的鸟类，更容易继续产生新的省级纪录；这一格局与分布边缘扩展、间歇性出现以及既有分布边缘区域被重新观察到等过程是一致的。迁徙性也是显著正向因子：与留鸟或低移动性类群相比，部分迁徙和完全迁徙鸟类更容易产生新的省级纪录。相比之下，体重和粗化后的风险组在当前鸟类数据中并未显示出稳定预测力。

综合来看，Figure 3 说明中国鸟类新纪录更容易出现在移动性强、分布范围广的类群中。也就是说，当前鸟类新纪录并不只是“稀有类群被重新发现”的结果，更可能同时反映了广布迁徙性类群的真实再分配过程和较高探测优势。

### Figure 4

省级层分析显示，在对新纪录数量按面积进行标准化之后，当前调查努力是驱动新纪录发现强度的关键正向因子。当前观测/报告强度越高的省份，其对数转换后的新纪录密度也越高。与此同时，省域面积呈显著负效应，说明大面积省份虽然绝对记录总数可能较高，但单位面积上的发现强度往往较低。历史调查努力、人均 GDP 和栖息地异质性在主模型中没有表现出强烈的独立效应，不过层次分解结果表明 GDP 仍占有一定比例的解释度。

相对贡献分析进一步说明了这一省级信号的结构：面积是解释度最大的变量，其次是当前调查努力，而历史努力和栖息地异质性的独立贡献相对较小。带面积 offset 的计数敏感性模型给出的总体方向与主模型一致，即努力因子和空间尺度因子仍然构成解释鸟类新纪录省级差异的核心轴线。整体而言，省级结果支持这样一种解释：中国鸟类新纪录的累积既受观测过程强度影响，也受到空间尺度约束。
