# Bird directional plots: result summary
# 鸟类新纪录方向图：结果摘要

## Overview / 总体概况
- Total bird new-record species used in directional analysis: 598.
- 纳入方向分析的鸟类新纪录物种总数：598。
- Overall overlay figures include the top 6 orders ranked by species counts.
- overall 叠加图纳入新纪录物种数排名前 6 的鸟类目。
- Combined 4x4 figures include the top 16 orders ranked by species counts.
- combined 4x4 图纳入新纪录物种数排名前 16 的鸟类目。

## Overall dominant directions / 总体优势方向
- Top overall directions: East (210, 35.1%); Northeast (128, 21.4%); Southeast (69, 11.5%).
- 总体上占比最高的方向为：East（210，35.1%）；Northeast（128，21.4%）；Southeast（69，11.5%）。

## Included and omitted orders / 纳入与舍弃的目
- Included overlay orders: Passeriformes, Charadriiformes, Accipitriformes, Anseriformes, Pelecaniformes, Columbiformes.
- Included 4x4 orders: Passeriformes, Charadriiformes, Accipitriformes, Anseriformes, Pelecaniformes, Columbiformes, Gruiformes, Coraciiformes, Piciformes, Strigiformes, Cuculiformes, Galliformes, Ciconiiformes, Gaviiformes, Suliformes, Caprimulgiformes.
- Dropped small-count orders: Procellariiformes, Podicipediformes, Falconiformes, Otidiformes, Phoenicopteriformes, Pterocliformes, Trogoniformes.
- 纳入 overall 叠加的目：Passeriformes、Charadriiformes、Accipitriformes、Anseriformes、Pelecaniformes、Columbiformes。
- 纳入 combined 4x4 的目：Passeriformes、Charadriiformes、Accipitriformes、Anseriformes、Pelecaniformes、Columbiformes、Gruiformes、Coraciiformes、Piciformes、Strigiformes、Cuculiformes、Galliformes、Ciconiiformes、Gaviiformes、Suliformes、Caprimulgiformes。
- 舍弃的小样本目：Procellariiformes、Podicipediformes、Falconiformes、Otidiformes、Phoenicopteriformes、Pterocliformes、Trogoniformes。

## Output structure / 输出结构
- `figures/overall`: overlay radar and overlay wind-rose figures based on major orders.
- `figures/combined`: 4x4 order-panel radar and wind-rose figures.
- `figures/radar_by_order` and `figures/windrose_by_order`: individual order-specific plots.
- `figures/overall`：主要目叠加的 radar 和 wind-rose 图。
- `figures/combined`：4x4 按目组合 radar 和 wind-rose 图。
- `figures/radar_by_order` 和 `figures/windrose_by_order`：各目单独图。
