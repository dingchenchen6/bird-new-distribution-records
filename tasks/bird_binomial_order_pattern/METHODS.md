# Methods | 方法说明

## Objective | 目标
Evaluate whether the observed proportion of bird new records in each order deviates from the expectation under a binomial reference model.
检验各鸟类目中新纪录观测比例是否偏离二项分布参考模型下的期望值。

## Workflow | 分析流程
1. Derive the total species pool and new-record species counts for each order.
2. Compute the expected proportion and the 95 percent binomial envelope across increasing sample size.
3. Plot observed order-level points on top of the confidence envelope.
4. Export figure, underlying plot data, and the envelope table.

1. 计算各目物种总池及新纪录物种数。
2. 基于递增样本量计算期望比例和95%二项分布包络线。
3. 将各目观测点叠加到置信包络图上。
4. 导出图件、作图数据和包络线数据。
